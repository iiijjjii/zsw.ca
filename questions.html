Why would you choose to work in cubicles?

Cubicles are often the scorn of office design so why would we voluntarily use them? Well they actually are pretty effective. The cubicles are space efficient. They cut down on noise. The walls seem to help with focus. They are modular. They have no drawers which forces one to be organized. AC power and ethernet are at desk height.
